japanese

私は勉べん強きょうするためにIt expresses a purpose rather than an action to do something, that’s why it’s better for me to use するために instead of しに. 私が知しらない漢かん字じにふりがなを書かきます。

こんにちは！皆みなさん大だい丈じょう夫ぶですか。

小さい漢字を読むことが難むずかしいですから、フォントのサイズを17pxにセットしました設せっ定ていしました is more natural and better for formal conversations, but why not use the power of japanglish when it is understandable and casual 😅。

このブログに初はじめて日本語で書いていますよ。日本語はまだ上手じょうずじゃないから、このテキストを書くのはI learned it is more natural to use 書くのは instead of 書くことが to stress the difficulty in writing in japanese, not the writing action itself.私には難むずかしいですが本当ほんとに楽たのしいです。

日本語の先生せんせいは宿題しゅくだいを出だしました。主題しゅだい主題 means “subject, theme”.は『源げん氏じ物もの語がたり源氏物語 means The Story of Genji. 源氏 is Genji (Kanji’s of source and Mr/Mrs), and 物語 means story. 物 means thing, and 語 means word, speach (often used as 語り). は 何ですか？説せつ明めい説明 means “explanation, description”.する。その内ない容ようIt means “contents, content”. を簡かん単たんIt means “simple, easy, uncomplicated”. に要よう約やくIt means “summary, digest”.します。』です。

それから、始はじDo not confuse 始 with 初. Although they have some overlapping meanings, 始 means “start, begin” whereas 初 means “first, at first”.めましょう！下に書きます。

源氏物語の要約

源氏物語は1003年ねんに紫むらさき式しき部ぶは書いた物語です。源氏物語は世せ界かいでIn these kind of contexts, で must be used with 世せ界かい.一番ばん古い小しょう説せつだDon’t forget だ! Nouns get a だ in the end in the context of plain form.と言います。内容は光ひかる源げん氏じHikaru Genji, the main protagonist of The Tale of Genjiという男おとこの人の物語です。光源氏はハンサムでDon’t forget to use で to connect a な-adjective with another adjective. Also remember that い-adjectives drop their い and gets くて instead.多た才さいIt means “talented”.な人です。彼かれは皇こう帝ていIt means “emperor”.の息むす子こIt means “son”.です。物語では、源氏は宮きゅう廷ていIt means “royal court”.で恋こいThe verb for “to fall in love”.をしたりたり～たり　form、政せい治じIt means “politics”.に関かかわっ関わる means “to be involved (in)”たりします。源氏は理り想そう的てき理り想そう的てき means “ideal”.な愛を求もとめ求もとめる means “to want, to request, to seek (as in this context)“.ながら～ながら means “while, during”. You can form a verb with ながら by taking its ます form, dropping the ます and replacing it with ながら.、自じ分ぶん自じ身しんThere is a little nuance there. 自じ分ぶん and 自じ身しん both mean “oneself”. 自分 itself is casual, 自身 is formal, and 自分自身 has the strongest emphasis among them.にも周しゅう囲いIt means “surroundings”. In this context, 周囲の人々 means “people around (him)”の人々ひとびと人 means “person”, 人々 means “people”. The kanji 々 (kurikaeshi) makes the kanji before itself read twice, often with the second part getting a dakuten at the first syllable. It usually adds a meaning of the thing being more than one.にも苦くるしみIt means “pain, suffering”.を与あたえます与える means “to give (something non-material like damage, influence, pain), to provide, to assign, to cause, to pass”.。源氏の死し後ご死し means “death”, 後ご means “after”. In this context, 源氏の死後 means “After the death of Genji”.、物語は彼かれの子し孫そんIt means “descendant”.の人じん生せいIt means “(one’s) life, human life”.に続つづきます続く means “to continue”.。特とくにIt means “particularly”.、孫まごIt means “grandchild”.のの can be used as “of, the” as in “Kaoru the grandchild”薫かおるIt normally read as 薫くん, and means “pleasent smell, aroma”, but in this context, it is the name of Kaoru Genji, the grandchild of Hikaru Genji.と彼が巻まき込こまれる巻き込む means “to caught up in”. It means “to be caught up in” when conjugated to ～られる form (Passive form).複ふく雑ざつIt means “complex”.な恋れん愛あい恋 is love that is youthful, emotional, often unfulfilled. 愛 is love that is serious, lasting, broader than just romance. 恋愛 is a neutral term for love/romance as a concept.三さん角かく関かん係けい三角 means “triangle, triangular shape”, and 関係 means “relationship”. 三角関係 has a broader meaning, that’s why combining it with 恋愛 as 恋愛三角関係 emphasizes the romantic context of it being a “love triangle”. At least, that’s how I understand it so far 😅に焦しょう点てんが当あてられます。焦点が当てられる roughly means “focus is placed on”. 焦点 means “focus, focal point”, and 当てる means “to hit, to apply, to direct (e.g. attention)”

難しかったがたくさんのI learned that I can’t modify a noun with a quantity adverb without の particle.ことを習ならいました！とても楽しかった、でも今疲つかれていますよ😮‍💨。じゃあ、また。👋
